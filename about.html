About Us

Through scientific study, educational initiatives, and community engagement, we seek to safeguard polar bears, their cubs, and the sea ice ecosystem they rely on for survival. Our objective is to ensure that polar bears will always be a part of the Arctic.

Our Works

Our far-reaching impact on habitat protection, restoration, and management has rescued several species from extinction, including eagles, deer, elk, bighorn sheep, and whales. Nonetheless, in our quickly changing world, our nation's wildlife continues to suffer grave threats. As we have done with other species, we are now concentrating our efforts on polar bears while our network of partners maintains our other activities.
